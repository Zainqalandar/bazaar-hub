# BazaarHub - Team Onboarding

## Project Objective
- Build a multi-vendor e-commerce platform with a strong backend architecture
- Support real-world e-commerce workflows
- Maintain clean role separation (Customer / Seller / Admin)

## Roles Overview
- Customer: shopping, orders, reviews
- Seller: product and order management
- Admin: platform control and analytics
